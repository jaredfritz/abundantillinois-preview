Key BUILD Bills

Our coalition supports each of the following BUILD bills:

Comprehensive Housing Reforms

HB 5626: BUILD PACKAGE (OMNIBUS)

Advances a comprehensive set of reforms including middle housing, ADUs, streamlined permitting, right-sized parking standards, and impact-fee consistency to expand housing supply statewide.

Middle Housing

SB 4060: MUNI CD-MIDDLE HOUSING

Legalizes middle-housing options on residential lots statewide and expands choice for duplexes, triplexes, fourplexes, and cottage clusters.

Building Codes - Stairs

SB 4061: CTY/MUNI BUILDING CODES-STAIRS

Enables single-stair multifamily buildings up to six stories with strong life-safety standards, supporting more practical small-site housing.

Impact Fee Standards

SB 4062: IMPACT FEE STANDARDS

Creates a consistent statewide framework for impact-fee calculations and public reporting, improving predictability for housing development.

Plans and Inspections

SB 4063: MUNI CD-BUILDING PLANS/INSPECT

Creates clearer review timelines and allows qualified third-party review or inspection to keep housing projects moving predictably.

Residential Parking

SB 4064: MUNI CD-RESIDENTIAL PARKING

Right-sizes parking standards so housing can be delivered at lower cost and with more flexibility for different project types.

Accessory Dwellings

SB 4071: MUNI CD-ACCESSORY DWELLINGS

Expands opportunities for ADUs across single-family areas, supporting gentle density and more housing choices for homeowners and renters.
