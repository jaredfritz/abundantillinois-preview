Key BUILD Bills

Core legislation in the BUILD package focuses on these statewide reform tracks:

Missing-Middle Housing

Allows more duplexes, triplexes, and fourplexes in areas limited to single-unit zoning.

Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs)

Expands legal pathways for backyard cottages, basement apartments, and coach houses.

Parking and Lot Standards

Reduces minimum parking and lot-size barriers that increase housing costs and delay projects.

Process and Permitting Clarity

Improves predictability for approvals and implementation timelines across municipalities.

Bill numbers and hearing dates will be posted here as the package moves through committee.
